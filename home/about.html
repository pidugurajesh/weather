About Us


Welcome to our weather forecasting website WEATHERINGwithU! We are a team of students, our mission is to provide accurate and up-to-date information about the weather conditions in your area and beyond.

As students, we may not have the same level of experience as professional meteorologists, but we are dedicated to learning and improving our forecasting skills. Our goal is to gain practical experience in the field of weather forecasting while providing good service to the users. We understand that weather plays a crucial role in our daily lives, from planning outdoor activities to ensuring personal safety. That's why we are committed to delivering accurate and timely forecasts to keep you informed and prepared.

Thank you for choosing this weather forecasting website. We appreciate your support!!!
